01
Concept

The project launches a series of thematic tokens, each representing a specific social idea or value. It is a platform where everyone can find views that resonate with them and become part of their growth, influence, and story.

02
Philosophy

We start from a simple principle: words are cheap. In a world of information noise, only action has true value.

Our mission is to give people a tool for genuine expression. We believe the only voice that cannot be ignored is one backed by action. Even a minimal financial contribution to an idea is a tangible act of support that speaks louder than any declaration. "notalone.you" is designed to separate the signal from the noise and reveal which ideas truly matter to people.

03
Flow

The process is built on four transparent stages:

  1. Issuance: We launch a token dedicated to a specific social concept (e.g., "soon" — about the value of the present moment).
  2. Support: People who share the idea support it by acquiring the token.
  3. Analysis: We collect and analyze anonymized data on token activity and distribution.
  4. Visualization: Based on this data, we create a "Value Map" — a clear reflection of which ideas resonate most with the community.
04
Roadmap

MOVE 1: Launch and Community Building

  • Issuance of the first token and its integration with key DeFi platforms (pump.fun, Dexscreener).
  • Establishment and growth of community hubs on Discord and Telegram.

MOVE 2: Technology Stack Development

  • Launch of a web platform for each token, allowing participants to share opinions and stories.
  • Implementation of a "story generator" — a tool that contextualizes and explains each social idea.
  • Creation of a notification system for subscribers about future launches.
  • Implementation of personal accounts linked to users' crypto wallets.

MOVE 3: Ecosystem Scaling

  • Launch of subsequent tokens with new social concepts.
  • Integration with popular social media platforms to expand reach.

MOVE 4: The Public "Value Map"

  • Launch of the interactive "Value Map" — the project's flagship product.
  • Providing public access to real-time analytics and data.
05
Investments

The project's economic model is based not just on investment, but on a contribution to the long-term stability of the entire ecosystem.

The Core Mechanism:

30% of the fees from trading operations on all social tokens are used to buy back the ecosystem's core token — YNA (You Not Alone).

A Guarantee of Long-Term Growth:

All YNA tokens acquired this way are locked for 5 years. We commit not to sell them during this period.

This creates a powerful dual effect:

  1. Constant Demand: It ensures continuous buying pressure on the YNA token, fostering its organic value growth.
  2. Long-Term Confidence: The five-year lock is our public guarantee of a long-term vision and commitment to the project. It is the foundation that provides stability and predictability for all ecosystem participants.
