## Metrics to Track

### Primary (North Star)
- **3+ Day Holders per Token** - True believers, not speculators
- **YNA Accumulated & Locked** - Ecosystem health
- **Holder Overlap** - How many hold multiple tokens (shared values)

### Secondary
- Channel subscribers/followers
- Engagement rate (comments, shares, reposts)
- Website traffic
- Social sharing kit usage

### Vanity (Nice to have)
- Total token holders
- Trading volume
- Social media impressions

## Brand Voice

**Do:**
- Be direct and honest
- Focus on philosophy and meaning
- Use minimal, clean language
- Celebrate genuine commitment
- Highlight transparency

**Don't:**
- Use crypto hype language ("moon", "diamond hands")
- Make price predictions
- Overpromise features
- Spam or push aggressively
- Compromise philosophical integrity
